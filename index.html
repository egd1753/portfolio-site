About Me

Hello, my name is Ethan Dugas. I'm currently residing in Lafayette, Louisiana, am an outdoor enthusiast, and a full-stack web developer currently looking for work. My background is in political science, management, and the financial industry, but in September of 2018 I decided to pursue software development and enrolled in Bloc's Web Development bootcamp.

Bloc's coursework and weekly mentor meetings with industry experts taught me how to be a great programmer and a career-minded developer. I'm passionate about creating the best experience for both users and other developers, though collaboration, creative problem-solving, and clean code practices. I'm excited to work in this industry and to bring my previous work experience and love of learning with me.
