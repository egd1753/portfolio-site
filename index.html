About Me

Hello, and welcome to my website! My name is Ethan Dugas, I love the outdoors, and I'm a full-stack web developer currently looking for work. I have a Bachelor's degree in political science and experience in management and the financial industry, but in September of 2018 I decided to pursue software development and enrolled in Bloc's Web Development bootcamp.

Bloc's coursework and weekly mentor meetings with industry experts taught me how to be a great programmer and a career-minded developer. I'm excited to work in this industry, and to bring my previous work experience and love of learning with me.
